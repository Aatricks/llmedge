Place your embedding model files here:

- all-minilm-l6-v2/model.onnx
- all-minilm-l6-v2/tokenizer.json

You can download from:
https://huggingface.co/sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2/tree/main (onnx/model.onnx and tokenizer.json)

At runtime, the RAGEngine copies these assets to internal storage before initializing the embedding model.
